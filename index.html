Biography

I am Tamer Ebeid, the Director of Corporate Quality at Beyti, Egypt’s leading dairy and juice producer under the Almarai group. With over 20 years of experience in quality assurance, I focus on ensuring the highest standards of safety, quality, and compliance in food production. At Beyti, I oversee laboratory operations and ensure adherence to international standards such as ISO 17025 and ISO 22000. My role involves managing the entire production process, ensuring that every product meets strict quality and safety requirements. I’m passionate about leading teams and driving continuous improvement to enhance product quality. Based in New Cairo, I remain committed to maintaining our reputation for excellence in the food industry.
